# Supply-Chain-Management
This project analyzes supply chain operations including logistics, inventory, suppliers, transportation, and sales performance from 2020–2025. The dashboard helps businesses track delivery efficiency, transportation cost, inventory management, and profitability across different regions and product categories. It also helps identify delayed shipments, high-cost suppliers, inventory imbalance, and seasonal sales trends, enabling better operational planning and data-driven business decisions.
The dashboard provides clear insights into supplier performance, transport delays, sales fluctuations, and inventory movement, helping management improve operational efficiency and reduce overall supply chain costs. Interactive filters and visualizations make it easier to monitor performance trends and support faster strategic decision-making.
